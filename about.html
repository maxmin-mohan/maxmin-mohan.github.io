Hello, I'm Mohan Kumar

A passionate video editor with over 5 years of experience in creating cinematic stories that capture life's most precious moments.

My journey in video editing began with a simple desire to preserve memories in the most beautiful way possible. What started as a hobby has evolved into a professional career where I help people tell their stories through the power of visual storytelling.

I specialize in wedding videos, event coverage, and special occasions, bringing a unique blend of technical expertise and creative vision to every project. My goal is to create videos that not only document moments but evoke emotions and tell compelling stories.
