Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer a comprehensive range of video editing services including:

  • Wedding cinematography and highlight reels
  • Corporate video editing and promotional content
  • Event coverage and documentation
  • Social media content optimization
  • Music video editing
  • Color grading and visual effects

How long does it take to complete a video project?

Turnaround times vary based on project complexity:

  • Wedding highlight (3-5 minutes): 1-2 weeks
  • Full ceremony edit (1-2 hours): 2-3 weeks
  • Corporate videos: 1-2 weeks
  • Social media content: 2-4 business days
  • Rush delivery available for additional fee

What's your pricing structure?

Pricing depends on several factors:

  • Project duration and complexity
  • Required turnaround time
  • Special effects or advanced editing needs
  • Amount of raw footage
  • Number of final deliverables

Contact me for a custom quote based on your specific requirements.

What formats and quality options do you offer?

I deliver videos in various formats and qualities:

  • 4K Ultra HD (3840x2160)
  • Full HD (1920x1080)
  • HD (1280x720)
  • Web-optimized versions
  • Social media platform-specific formats
  • Multiple file formats: MP4, MOV, AVI

What's your revision policy?

My revision policy ensures your satisfaction:

  • 3 rounds of revisions included in base price
  • 48-hour turnaround for revision requests
  • Additional revisions available at hourly rate
  • Clear communication of changes needed
  • Detailed feedback tracking system

How do we get started?

The process is simple:

  1. Fill out the contact form with project details
  2. Schedule a free consultation call
  3. Receive a custom quote and timeline
  4. Sign agreement and pay initial deposit
  5. Begin the creative process

What about file delivery and backup?

I ensure secure and reliable delivery:

  • Secure cloud delivery via Google Drive or Dropbox
  • Physical USB/hard drive delivery available
  • All projects backed up for 6 months
  • Extended backup services available
  • Download links valid for 30 days
